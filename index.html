PROJECTS

    Research paper : Mechanical Characterization of Dates seed and Neem gum powder reinforced Polymer Composites

  • The primary objective of this project is the development of natural filler reinforced polymer laminates.
  • Crushed and powdered date seed powder and neem gum powder acts as a natural filler in the chosen epoxy matrix.
  • The laminates were prepared and subjected to various mechanical tests like tensile, flexural and impact test and surface test FESEM and fractography analysis by FTIR test
  • The obtained results were analysed and specimen 2 consisting 5% neem gum powder and 25%dates powder provide better strength than the other compositions.
